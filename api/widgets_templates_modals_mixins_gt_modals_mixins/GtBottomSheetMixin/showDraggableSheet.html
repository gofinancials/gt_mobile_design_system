Displays a bottom sheet that can be dragged up and down by the user.

The builder provides a ScrollController that must be attached to a scrollable widget within the sheet to enable the drag behavior. The sheet's size is constrained by minChildSize, initialChildSize, and maxChildSize, which represent fractions of the screen height.
